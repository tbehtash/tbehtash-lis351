Songs and Albums that Changed My Life

Decades of Music

My favorite music spans four distinct decades. For each decade, I have listed my favorite genres and associated artists.

60s
- Blues Rock: Elvis Presley
- Roots Rock: Creedence Clearwater Revival, Bob Dylan
- Rock: Jimi Hendrix, The Rolling Stones
70s
- Disco: The Bee Gees; Earth, Wind & Fire
- Psychedelic Rock: Pink Floyd
80s
- Pop: Tears for Fears, David Bowie, Billy Idol
90s
- Alternative Rock: The Red Hot Chili Peppers, Garbage, Weezer
- Worldwide: Israel Kamakawiwo'ole
